# CLAIM DISCIPLINE

Claim ladder:
- Level 0: No data.
- Level 1: Single session above chance.
- Level 2: Multiple sessions of same person above chance.
- Level 3: Multiple people replicate under same protocol.
- Level 4: Independent replication with stricter double-blind controls.
- Level 5: Only then theoretical discussion of interface/filter/receiver models.

Forbidden claim:
- At Level 1 or 2, do not claim proof of non-local consciousness.
