# SAFETY

Forbidden in ExperimentX protocol:
- no near-death simulation
- no oxygen deprivation
- no hyperventilation
- no drugs
- no sleep deprivation
- no medical self-experiments
- no panic induction

Additional safeguards:
- no testing children without ethics review and parental supervision
- stop sessions if psychological distress appears
- do not use results for life decisions
- participation must be voluntary, sober, and documented
